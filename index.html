To The One Who Stole My Heart

Love isn’t perfect, but it’s perfectly worth it. When love is true, it becomes a part of who you are. It inspires you to be better, to dream bigger, and to hold on through storms. It’s in the laughter after a hard day, the warmth in a glance, and the memories made with care. Real love is timeless — not because of grand moments, but because of the deep, quiet bond that never fades.
